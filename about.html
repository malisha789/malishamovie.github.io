Our Branches

FlickSphere Cinemas is proud to have multiple locations across the country, offering the ultimate movie experience.

Colombo City Centre

Located in the heart of Colombo, this flagship branch features 5 premium screens, Dolby Atmos sound, and VIP lounges.

Kandy Central

Kandy Central provides a luxurious movie-going experience in the Hill Capital, ideal for families and students alike.

Galle Bay View

Enjoy your favorite movies with a seaside view at our Galle Bay View branch, the newest addition to our family.
