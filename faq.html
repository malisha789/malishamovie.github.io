Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book tickets online?

Yes! You can book tickets online through our website and mobile app.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are only issued for cancellations made at least 3 hours before the showtime.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount for students with valid ID during weekdays.

Are outside food and drinks allowed?

Unfortunately, no. We provide a wide range of snacks and beverages at our food stalls.
